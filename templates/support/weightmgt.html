Healthy Weight Management After Pregnancy

It's common for new mothers to focus on losing pregnancy weight. However, it’s important to approach weight management in a healthy and sustainable way.

Tips for Healthy Weight Loss:

  • Start with small changes, like adding more vegetables to your diet.
  • Focus on regular physical activity, such as walking or light stretching.
  • Listen to your body’s hunger cues and avoid restrictive diets.

Getting Support:

Consider working with a nutritionist or joining a support group for new moms to stay motivated and supported on your weight management journey.
